# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 14, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Discord bots (collectively, "the Bots") collect, use, store, and protect information when you interact with them. By using the Bots, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to transparency and to handling your information responsibly. We only collect what is necessary to operate the Bots effectively.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 User IDs

When you interact with the Bots, we may collect and store your **Discord User ID**. This is a unique numerical identifier assigned to your Discord account by Discord. We do **not** collect:

- Your username or display name (beyond what Discord provides at the time of interaction)
- Your email address
- Your IP address
- Any payment or billing information
- Any personal information outside of Discord

### 2.2 Server / Guild Data

We may collect and store data related to the Discord servers ("guilds") in which the Bots operate. This may include:

- **Server (Guild) IDs** — unique numerical identifiers for Discord servers.
- **Channel IDs** — to identify specific channels where the Bots are configured to operate.
- **Role IDs** — where role-based permissions or features are configured.
- **Server configuration settings** — preferences or settings that server administrators configure for the Bots.

We do **not** collect or log general message content from server members.

---

## 3. How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

- **Provide and operate** the Bot's features and functionality.
- **Store server-specific configurations** set by server administrators.
- **Track usage permissions** and ensure appropriate access control.
- **Improve the Bots** by understanding how features are being used at an aggregate level.
- **Enforce our Terms of Service**, including investigating abuse or misuse.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's services:

- **User IDs** are retained as long as they are associated with active Bot functionality (e.g., saved preferences, permissions). They may be deleted upon request.
- **Server/Guild data** is retained for as long as the Bot remains in the server. When the Bot is removed from a server, associated server data may be deleted automatically or upon request.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the data we collect from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of the information stored.

We encourage server administrators to follow Discord's best practices for server security to further protect their communities.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

The Bots operate within the **Discord platform**, which has its own [Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms). We are not responsible for Discord's data practices. Please review Discord's policies to understand how they handle your data.

We do not use other third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services in connection with the Bots.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bots are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used the Bots in violation of this policy, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including:

- **Access** — the right to know what data we hold about you.
- **Deletion** — the right to request deletion of your data.
- **Correction** — the right to request correction of inaccurate data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our official Discord support server or contact channel. We will respond to requests within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the **Effective Date** at the top of this document. Continued use of the Bots after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

---

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our official Discord support server or contact channel.
